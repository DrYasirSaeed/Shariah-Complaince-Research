Research Methodology & Data Construction Note1. Data Source and Sample PeriodThe longitudinal dataset for this study is meticulously constructed from the State Bank of Pakistan (SBP) annual publications titled “Financial Statements Analysis of All Listed Non-Financial Companies.”Time Horizon: 2005 – 2023 (19-Year Balanced/Unbalanced Panel)Scope: All non-financial firms listed on the Pakistan Stock Exchange (PSX).Unit of Analysis: Firm-year observations.2. The Multi-Schema ChallengeA primary methodological contribution of this pipeline is the resolution of structural shifts in SBP reporting standards. Over the 19-year span, the SBP revised its data hierarchy and formula brackets three times. To maintain longitudinal integrity, three distinct computational engines were developed to map these shifting schemas:Reporting PeriodSchema CharacteristicsKey Adaptation Strategy2005 – 2008Legacy SBP formatExplicit mapping of primary sub-component sums.2009 – 2013Transitional formatRe-anchoring of non-current liability variables.2014 – 2023Modern IFRS-aligned formatFuzzy-matching engine for dynamic variable sub-brackets.3. Variable OperationalizationThe following Shariah-screening and profitability ratios were computed using standardized logic across all reporting eras.3.1 Profitability MetricReturn on Assets (ROA): Measures the firm's efficiency in using its assets to generate net earnings.$$ROA = \frac{\text{Profit After Tax}}{\text{Total Assets}}$$3.2 Shariah Screening Ratios (KMI-30/AAOIFI Standards)Debt Ratio (DR): Evaluates the level of interest-bearing debt relative to total assets.$$DR = \frac{\text{Long-Term Debt} + \text{Short-Term Borrowings}}{\text{Total Assets}}$$Investment Ratio (IR): Assesses the proportion of non-compliant investments.$$IR = \frac{\text{Short-Term Investments}}{\text{Total Assets}}$$Income Ratio (IncR): Captures the percentage of non-permissible (other) income.$$IncR = \frac{\text{Non-Compliant (Other) Income}}{\text{Total Sales}}$$Illiquid Asset Ratio (IAR): Determines the ratio of physical/real assets to total assets.$$IAR = \frac{\text{Non-Current Assets} + \text{Inventories}}{\text{Total Assets}}$$Net Liquid Assets (NLA): A strict liquidity measure relative to share capital.$$NLA = \frac{\text{Total Assets} - \text{Illiquid Assets} - \text{Total Liabilities}}{\text{Total Number of Outstanding Shares}}$$4. Computational Logic & Data Cleaning4.1 Fuzzy-Logic Variable MatchingThe SBP frequently updated formula brackets within their Excel publications (e.g., transitioning "Non-Current Assets (A2+A3)" to "(A1+A3+A4+A5+A6)").Solution: The Python pipeline employs a root-string search algorithm that identifies the core variable (e.g., "Non-Current Assets") within the item descriptions, ensuring the correct aggregate is captured regardless of the specific sub-component count.4.2 Null-Safe Mathematics ("Safe Addition")In financial datasets, blank entries often represent zero exposure (e.g., a debt-free firm). Standard programming logic often treats Number + Null as Null, potentially leading to massive data loss.Solution: A custom safe_add function ensures that null values are treated as $0$ during additive operations, while preserving the null status for divisions to prevent inaccurate results.4.3 Data Normalization ParserTo ensure the dataset is "Model-Ready," raw SBP data underwent the following transformations:Sign Conversion: Converted accounting notation (500) into valid floats -500.0.String Sanitization: Percentage strings (e.g., "5.5%") and thousand-separators were stripped and converted to decimal format.5. Sequence and Hierarchy PreservationTo maintain the economic context of the Pakistan Stock Exchange, this study avoids standard alphabetical sorting which disrupts sector-specific hierarchies.Strategy: The final master dataset was merged using Categorical Sequencing, adhering to the original SBP Sector/Sub-Sector order.Result: This preserves the industry-wise grouping, allowing for more nuanced sectorial analysis and peer-group comparisons.
